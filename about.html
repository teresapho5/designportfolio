So, you might be wondering what I like to do, what my hobbies are, passions, etc. To give you a bit of insight of who I am, I am a healthcare advocate, social impact activist, design/videography/VR connoisseur, and a never-ending learner. The intersections between several disciplines excite me! Why? Because every day, it's like we're piecing together a puzzle, in order to better understand our world, better improve the lives of those around us, and find what passions make us alive and bring the best out of us!

With my extracurriculars, I chose to really focus around projects that involve a ton of exposure and interaction among people. I grew to enjoy working with people ranging from low-income populations to many patients through various healthcare opportunities (clinical work, hospitalized patients, etc). Whether I'm working with hospitalized patients to working on side projects to help the visually disabled, I am passionate in contributing back to my community in that aspect!

Prior to this, I was pre-optometry for half my entire college career due to my interest in physics, optics, and patient interaction. However, as a child, I loved tinkering with different softwares and programs as someone who didn't grow up with a lot of resources. But with one computer, it allowed me to explore the digital world and experiment with a variety of designs mediums, style, and tools.

So with an undying love for design and optics, I eventually found a new interesting between the interesections this past summer, which is VR!
